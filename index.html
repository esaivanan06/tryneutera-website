About

We are an early-stage electronics product company focused on building indigenous satcom and networking infrastructure. Our work spans system-level engineering—from hardware design to firmware integration—ensuring deep technical ownership and long-term scalability.

Positioned within Tamil Nadu’s innovation ecosystem and aligned with national initiatives such as Make in India, we are actively developing secure, future-ready communication systems, including work on satellite communication modules in collaboration with government stakeholders.

Vision

6G Backhaul Solutions

Data Centers in Space

6G Radio Access Networks (RAN)

Mission

To design and build indigenous electronic systems that enable secure, scalable, and future-ready satcom and networking infrastructure.
