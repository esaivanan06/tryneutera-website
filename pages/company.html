Company

Tryneutera Electronics Private Limited is an early-stage electronics product company focused on building indigenous telecom and networking infrastructure.

Founded with the objective of strengthening India’s electronic self-reliance, Tryneutera works across system-level engineering— from hardware design to firmware integration and manufacturing alignment.

The company operates with a long-term vision, prioritizing engineering depth, design ownership, and scalability over short-term market expansion.

Mission

To design and build indigenous electronic systems that enable secure, scalable, and future-ready telecom and networking infrastructure.
